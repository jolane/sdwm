What we do

Simply put, we’re problem solvers. Our process for delivering creative solutions is simple and gymnast level flexible. We can give you the works, or you can pick and mix the services you need. If you don’t need us for all three of these areas that’s a-OK, but we still highly recommend that you have them covered to give your brand the best chance of success.

Perhaps the most important step in building any brand, this is the creation of the blueprint that all communication activity will follow. We establish why and where a brand should sit in the market, and create a strategy for its growth.
  • Research and understand the consumer
  • Assess the market and industry
  • Review the competition
  • Define the target market
  • Highlight relevant trends and insights
  • Establish brand purpose
  • Define potential brand territories
  • Define brand challenge
  • Creation of a master brand strategy document

This is where creativity comes into play to bring brand personality to life. Here we create the assets every brand needs to effectively communicate. How it looks, feels, talks and behaves, and its very reason to exist are defined in this phase.
  • Brand platform creation
  • Identity design
  • Brand style Guide
  • Packaging
  • Tone of voice
  • Naming
  • Conceptual thinking
  • Product development
  • Business ideas
  • Creative workshops
